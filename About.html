About

Here at Cj's Auto we have dedicated mechanics that makes sure they tell you what is wrong with your vehicle and not play the guessing game! We started with elementary school best friends with no automotive experience at all just working on our own cars and family cars. On day, we all decided to go get certified so we can open our own shop!

We all got certified then started putting out flyers to gather some more customers that weren't family! We then saved our money and put it all together to get our business license and find out own shop.
